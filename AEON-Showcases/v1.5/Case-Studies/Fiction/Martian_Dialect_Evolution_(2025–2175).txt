<size=120%><b>TIMELINE SIMULATION</b></size>

### <b>PHASE 1: SETTLEMENT ERA (2025-2050)</b>
<i>Generation Zero: The Founders</i>

<b>Phonological shifts begin:</b>
- Dome acoustics favor vowel raising and fronting
- Pressurized environment creates slight nasalization
- Low gravity affects breath support → elongated vowels

<b>Early lexical innovations:</b>
- "dust" → <b>dustfall</b> (Martian dust storm)
- "Earth" → <b>Blueworld</b> (visual reference)
- "EVA" → <b>outside</b> (simplification, verb: "to outside")
- "sol" (Martian day) fully replaces "day" in time-keeping
- <b>redshift</b> = homesickness for Earth
- <b>skipper</b> = person born on Earth (low gravity gait)
- <b>dome-echo</b> = recognizing someone by voice reverb

<b>Sample phrases:</b>
- "Three sols till dustfall, check your seals."
- "She's gone outside for the water run."
- "Getting redshift bad this winter."

---

### <b>PHASE 2: DIVERGENCE (2050-2090)</b>
<i>Generation One & Two: First Mars-born adults</i>

<b>Major phonological changes:</b>
- /θ/ and /ð/ merge with /f/ and /v/ (ث → ف shift)
  - "the" → <i>ve</i>, "think" → <i>fink</i>
- Long vowels diphthongize: 
  - "dome" → <i>dəʊm</i> → <i>dowm</i>
  - "home" → <i>həʊm</i> → <i>howm</i>
- Gravity-influenced prosody: Rising final intonation becomes default (query-speak)

<b>Identity lexicon emerges:</b>
- <b>Redborn</b> = Mars-native (first generation)
- <b>Dustlung</b> = veteran colonist (20+ years)
- <b>Greener</b> = recent Earth arrival (derogatory)
- <b>Scatter</b> = diaspora to new domes
- <b>Under-sky</b> = being outside (poetic)
- <b>Breathlock</b> = airlock (semantic shift)

<b>Tech/geography terms:</b>
- <b>Valles</b> (Valles Marineris) → <b>Ve Valleys</b>
- <b>Olympus</b> (Mons) → <b>Ve Mountain</b>
- Phobos/Deimos → <b>Fastmoon</b>/<b>Slowmoon</b>
- <b>Permstruct</b> = permanent habitat
- <b>Tempshell</b> = temporary shelter
- <b>Redline</b> = mission danger threshold

<b>Ritual language develops:</b>
- <b>Pre-EVA blessing:</b> "Air tight, spirit bright, red dust waits"
- <b>Return formula:</b> "Dowm safe, dowm sound"
- <b>Memorial phrase:</b> "Gone to ve valleys" (death)

<b>Sample conversation:</b>
```
"Ve new greener doesn't fink we should outside in vis dustfall."
"Typical skipper talk. Redborns know ve redline."
"My dustlung says we wait free sols minimum."
```

### <b>PHASE 3: CONSOLIDATION (2090-2130)</b>
<i>Generation Three & Four: Mars identity crystallizes</i>

<b>Phonological stabilization:</b>
- /r/ weakens intervocalically: "Mars" → <i>Mahs</i> / <i>Moss</i>
- Cluster reduction: "tempshell" → <i>tempsh</i>, "outside" → <i>outsi'</i>
- Distinct pitch accent system (dome-by-dome variation)

<b>Grammatical innovations:</b>
- <b>Progressive aspect marker:</b> "be-ing" → <b>-sa</b>
  - "I am working" → "I worksa"
- <b>Dome-specific pronouns:</b>
  - <b>wi</b> = we (dome collective, exclusive)
  - <b>wa</b> = we (Mars collective, inclusive)
- <b>Evidential marker:</b> <b>-ka</b> (Earth-knowledge) vs <b>-ma</b> (Mars-knowledge)
  - "It rains-ka on Blueworld" vs "Dustfalls-ma every winter"

<b>Expanded lexicon:</b>
- <b>Howmsa</b> = homesickness for Mars (used by diaspora)
- <b>Crater-clan</b> = extended family group
- <b>Sunwait</b> = seasonal affective disorder (Mars winter)
- <b>Bloodthin</b> = anemia (low gravity effect)
- <b>Brightside</b> = optimism (solar exposure)
- <b>Deeptime</b> = geological/planetary timescale thinking

<b>New compound formations:</b>
- <b>Redborn-fastmoon</b> = very quick (temporal compound)
- <b>Blueworld-greener</b> = hopelessly naive
- <b>Dustlung-wise</b> = experienced judgment

<b>Sample narrative:</b>
```
"Ve crater-clan meetsa in ve big dowm tonight. My dustlung-uncle talksa 
about ven ve first scatter, ven wi builded ve Valleys permstruct. 
He says-ma ve Blueworld greeners couldn't fink how wa survives-ma 
ve big dustfall of '87. But wi knows-ma ve redline, wi stays dowm safe."
```

### <b>PHASE 4: CRYSTALLIZATION (2130-2175)</b>
<i>Generation Five & Six: Distinct language variety</i>

<b>Full phonological system:</b>
- <b>Vowel inventory shifts:</b>
  - FACE: /eɪ/ → /æɪ/
  - GOAT: /oʊ/ → /ɔʊ/ 
  - PRICE: /aɪ/ → /ɑɪ/
  
- <b>Consonant changes:</b>
  - th → f/v (complete)
  - Glottal stop insertion dome-to-dome marker
  - /t/ → /ʔ/ word-finally: "wait" → <i>waiʔ</i>

<b>Distinctive grammar:</b>
- <b>Aspect system:</b>
  - <b>-sa</b>: ongoing (worksa)
  - <b>-pa</b>: completed (workpa)
  - <b>-ta</b>: future/hypothetical (workta)

- <b>Motion verbs:</b>
  - <b>outsi</b> = go outside/EVA
  - <b>insi</b> = return to dome
  - <b>upsi</b> = go to orbit
  - <b>downsi</b> = land from orbit

- <b>Honorific system:</b>
  - <b>Dust-</b> prefix for elders (Dust-Mara)
  - <b>Red-</b> prefix for first-generation Mars-born (Red-Chen)
  - <b>Green-</b> prefix for Earth-born (dismissive)

<b>Naming conventions:</b>

<b>Personal names:</b>
- <b>Earth names</b> + <b>Martian clan-markers:</b>
  - Zara Olymp (family from Olympus Mons region)
  - Kai Valless (Valles Marineris)
  - Mira Craterhollow
  
- <b>Pure Mars names (Gen 4+):</b>
  - Dustborn (unisex)
  - Redmoon (feminine)
  - Ironox (masculine)
  - Fastwing (unisex - rescue workers)

<b>Dome names evolved:</b>
- Armstrong Base → <b>Strongdowm</b>
- Jezero Station → <b>Waterdowm</b>  
- Olympus Colony → <b>Highdowm</b>

<b>Religious/ritual language:</b>

<b>Birth blessing:</b>
```
"Born of ve red dust, breathsa ve made air,
Walks-ta ve red ground, dustlung-grow fair.
Fastmoon rise, Slowmoon fall,
Vis child of Moss, redborn to all."
```

<b>EVA departure ritual:</b>
```
Leader: "Who goes-ta under-sky?"
Crew: "Wi goes-ta, red and ready."
Leader: "Vell ve redline hold?"
Crew: "Ve redline holds-ma in us."
All: "Air tight, spirit bright, dowm safe we return-ta."
```

<b>Death rite:</b>
```
"Gone to ve valleys, scattered to ve dust,
Blueworld-memory fades-pa, red ground is just.
No more outsi, no more insi,
Deeptime claims vem, crater-rest finally."
```
