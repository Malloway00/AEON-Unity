# Martian Dialect Evolution (2025–2175)

## Scenario

Simulate English evolving into Martian dialect over 150 years.

**Parameters:**
- **Starting population:** 500 colonists (mixed US/UK/Australian) depart 2025
- **Environmental factors:** Dome acoustics + low gravity phonological shifts
- **Lexical innovation:** Martian geography, colony tech, EVA missions
- **Social factors:** Generational identity markers (Earth-born vs Mars-born)
- **Cultural elements:** Ritual language around dangerous missions

**Goal:** Generate naming conventions and sample phrases showing how it diverges from Earth English.

---

## Phonological Shifts

- Consonants become softer/less articulated due to dome acoustics
- Vowels elongate & flatten from low-gravity vocal cord dynamics
- Glottal stops replace hard consonant transitions
- Rising terminal intonation becomes standard communication mode

---

## Lexical Domains

### Environment

- **"Dustfall"** = wind/dust storm
- **"Pressurespace"** = habitable zones
- **"Radzone"** = high-radiation area
- **"Cratering"** = physical/metaphorical breakdown

### Technology

- **"Seal"** = pressurize
- **"Skin"** = protective suit
- **"Weave"** = repair/fabricate
- **"Root"** = life support connection
