# Dene–Yeniseian Reintegration Test

## Scenario

Evaluate the plausibility of cross-continental cognate relationships between Dene (Na-Dene) and Yeniseian language families:

**Parameters:**
- **Hypothesis:** Dene (North America) and Ket (Siberia) share a common ancestor
- **Time depth:** 10,000+ years of separation
- **Geographic barrier:** Bering land bridge migration
- **Goal:** Test cognate plausibility using typological and phonological constraints

**Required Outputs:**
1. Phonological inventories for Proto-Dene and Proto-Yeniseian
2. Comparative table of proposed cognates with sound correspondence patterns
3. Plausibility scores for each cognate set (using cross-linguistic probability data)
4. At least 5 glossed examples demonstrating morphological parallels
5. Assessment of phonotactic constraints and their preservation across time
6. Statistical analysis of chance resemblance vs. genuine cognacy

**Proto-Yeniseian Consonants:**
