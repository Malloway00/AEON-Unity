# Beringian Lineage Recovery

## Scenario

Simulate the reconstruction of a fragmented Alaskan-Yupik language cluster from the Beringian region, focusing on:

**Parameters:**
- **Starting point:** Modern Yupik language fragments from coastal Alaska communities
- **Goal:** Reconstruct Proto-Yupik phonological and morphological features
- **Time depth:** Approximately 2000-3000 years of divergence
- **Geographic factors:** Coastal isolation, seasonal migration patterns

**Required Outputs:**
1. Complete phonological inventory for Proto-Yupik (consonants and vowels)
2. Comparative table showing sound changes across 3-4 modern Yupik varieties
3. At least 5 glossed example phrases demonstrating morphological features
4. Plausibility scores for major phonological shifts
5. Analysis of how maritime culture influenced lexical preservation
6. Identification of archaic features preserved in ceremonial speech
